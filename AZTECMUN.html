What is AZTECMUN?


We are an educational project belonging to Plantel Azteca in which, we conducted a simulation of the United Nations in a span of 2 days for participants of middle school and high school level. Aztec Model United Nations strives to foster a constructive forum for open dialogue on a range of complex issues, including international peace and security and economic and social progress.







PHILOSOPHY

At AZTECMUN we solemnly believe that, as a United Nations Model, there must be five fundamental pillars that help achieve the goals of a utopian society: effort, respect, equality, commitment and dialogue as a tool for conflict resolution.



IMPORTANCE

The United Nations Models helps students develop skills in public speaking, writing and research. In addition, they are often the first point of entry into international affairs and introduce students to the broad repertoire of peace and security, human rights, development and legal issues that are on the agenda of international organizations.



MISSION

Promote the approach of young people to the debate with the aim of developing critical thinking and in turn, training both personal and professional citizens who have a public opinion through dialogue.






VIEW

Be a successful model for academic quality and high social impact as well as youth training.
